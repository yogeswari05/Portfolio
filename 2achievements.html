My Achievements

I am delighted to announce that I have been chosen as a recipient of the prestigious NMMS scholarship, a testament to my academic prowess and commitment to excellence. Additionally, I achieved the top position in a poem recitation competition organized by Samskara Bharathi, showcasing my artistic flair and expressive abilities. Furthermore, I am honored to have been recognized as the Best Performer of the Year by the esteemed Rangostav Celebration Organization, highlighting my outstanding contributions and dedication to the arts. In acknowledgment of my overall excellence, I was bestowed with the Pratiba Puraskar, a distinguished award that celebrates exceptional achievements. Adding to my list of accomplishments, I am proud to have clinched the gold medal in the Kangaroo's Art Competition, an achievement that reflects my artistic skills and creative prowess. These accolades collectively underscore my commitment to academic and artistic excellence, and I am grateful for the opportunities to showcase my talents on various platforms.
